1 # Wizard of Wikipedia Data Collection Task
2 
3 The task involves two people holding a conversation. One dialog partner
4 chooses a topic to discuss, and then dialog proceeds.
5 
6 One partner is the Wizard, who has access to retrieved external
7 information conditioned on the last two utterances, as well as
8 information regarding the chosen topic.
9 
10 The other partner is the Apprentice, who assumes the role of someone
11 eager to learn about the chosen topic.
12 
13 This task was used to collect the dataset for the wizard_of_wikipedia task
14 contained in the tasks folder. A detailed description of the project may
15 be found in [Dinan et al. (ICLR 2019)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1811.01241).
16 For more details and pre-trained models, please see the
17 [Wizard of Wikipedia project page](https://github.com/facebookresearch/ParlAI/tree/master/projects/wizard_of_wikipedia).
18 The project page contains the Mechanical Turk task used to evaluate
19 pre-trained models in this paper.
