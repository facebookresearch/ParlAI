1 Task: Empathetic Dialogues
2 ===========================
3 Description: A dataset of 25k conversations grounded in emotional situations to facilitate training and evaluating dialogue systems. See https://arxiv.org/abs/1811.00207 for more information.
4 ===========================
5 Dataset has been released under the CC BY-NC license.
6 EmpatheticDialogueTeacher returns examples like so:
7 - [text]: context line (previous utterance by 'speaker')
8 - [labels]: label line (current utterance by 'listener')
9 with additional task specific fields:
10 - [situation]: a 1-3 sentence description of the situation that the conversation is
11 - [emotion]: one of 32 emotion words
12 Other optional fields:
13 - [prepend_ctx]: fasttext prediction on context line - or None
14 - [prepend_cand]: fasttext prediction on label line (candidate) - or None
15 - [deepmoji_ctx]: vector encoding from deepmoji penultimate layer - or None
16 - [deepmoji_cand]: vector encoding from deepmoji penultimate layer for label line (candidate) - or None
