23 class MultiWozTeacher(FixedDialogTeacher):
24  '''
25  MultiWOZ Teacher.
26  This dataset contains more than just dialogue. It also contains:
27  data.json also contains the following information:
28  1. information about the goal of the conversation (what the person is looking for)
29  2. topic of the conversation
30  3. log of the conversation (the conversation itself + metadata about each utterance)
31  Metadata: any taxi, police, restaurant, hospital, hotel, attraction, or train info mentioned
32  Information about each metadata category is also contained in its own json file.
33  1. restaurant.json: Restaurants + their attributes in the Cambridge UK area (address, pricerange, food...)
34  2. attraction.json: Attractions + their attributes in the Cambridge UK area (location, address, entrance fee...)
35  3. hotel_db.json: Hotels + their attributes in the Cambridge UK area (address, price, name, stars...)
36  4. train_db.json: Trains + their attributes in the Cambridge UK area (destination, price, departure...)
37  5. hospital_db.json: data about the Cambridge hospital's departments (department, phone, id)
38  6. police_db.json: Name address and phone number of Cambridge police station
39  7. taxi_db.json: Taxi information (taxi_colors, taxi_types, taxi_phone)
40  More information about the jsons can be found in readme.json
41  '''
